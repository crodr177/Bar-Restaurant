Our Story

Located at 1225 S. Main Street in the heart of the Downtown Las Vegas Arts District, it's the only bar where “Everything's For Sale!” Created by Las Vegas Arts District Board President, and former Atomic Liquors co-owner Derek Stonebarger, ReBAR has a great selection of local and domestic beers, classic bottled beers, draft wine, and Charitable Cocktails. Proceeds from the Charitable Cocktails menu are donated to Las Vegas Arts District, Nevada Preservation Foundation, The Huntridge Foundation, After-School All-Stars, Local Animal Charities, and Opportunity Village. Purchase any of John Mull's sausages on Great Buns Bakery pretzel rolls, and a portion is donated to Three Square Food Bank to help feed hungry Southern Nevadans. Eat a Sausage — Feed Yourself — And a Hungry Southern Nevadan At The Same Time.
